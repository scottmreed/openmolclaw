# What the hosted ChemIllusion product adds

OpenMolClaw is the open, local-first core: Ketcher, RDKit tools, a router-first
agent harness, skills, and explicit model-provider support — everything you
need to run chemistry agents from your own machine, with no ChemIllusion
account.

[ChemIllusion](https://chemillusion.com) is the hosted product built by the same
team. It layers polished teaching and authoring workflows, hosted frontier-model
access, and classroom delivery on top of the same chemistry-native foundation.
If OpenMolClaw is the engine, ChemIllusion is the finished vehicle.

Everything below is **hosted-only** — it is intentionally *not* part of the open
harness, and OpenMolClaw never requires any of it.

## Authoring & visualization

- **Graphical Abstract preparation** — publication-ready graphical abstracts from
  your structures and reactions.
- **Deck Creator** — generate teaching slide decks that follow a textbook's
  content sequence.
- **Course Creator / textbook builder** — assemble full course materials from
  structured chemistry content.
- **Journal Club** — turn a paper into a guided, presentation-ready session.
- **Commercial image and video generation** — animated mechanisms, molecule
  videos, and rich figures rendered by hosted models.

## Teaching & tutoring

- **ChemLingo** — interactive, language-aware chemistry tutoring for students.
- **Guided student workflows** — scaffolded practice, hints, and teacher-approved
  content paths.

## Classroom & delivery

- **LMS integration** — deliver ChemIllusion activities inside your learning
  management system, with roster and gradebook connectivity.
- **Teacher and student roles** — managed projects, sharing, and class-level
  organization.
- **Hosted persistence** — your projects, canvases, and generated assets saved
  and synced across devices.

## Models

- **Hosted frontier-model access** — the hosted product can call managed,
  higher-capability model providers for tasks that benefit from them, while
  OpenMolClaw stays fully local/open by design.
